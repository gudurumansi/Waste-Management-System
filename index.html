About

Waste management is a critical aspect of environmental sustainability, addressing the challenges of waste generation and its impact on ecosystems. This project introduces an innovative website that encourages households to manage plastic waste effectively. The platform enables users to collect and upload images of plastic items, connect with local traders for proper disposal, and earn rewards like deals, coupons, or cashback through partnered apps. By raising awareness about the importance of reducing, reusing, and recycling, and providing educational resources the platform fosters responsible waste disposal habits. Through seamless technology integration, this initiative promotes a circular economy, reduces the environmental footprint of waste, and transforms waste management into a rewarding and community-driven effort.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is waste management? ▶
Waste management refers to the collection, transportation, and disposal of waste materials in a systematic and efficient manner to reduce their impact on the environment and human health.
How do I practice waste management at home? ▶
Practicing waste management at home includes reducing waste generation, segregating waste at the source, composting organic waste, recycling, and properly disposing of hazardous waste.
What are the first few steps to initiate a waste management programme in your apartment complex? ▶
Initiating a waste management program in an apartment complex involves educating residents about the importance of waste management, setting up waste segregation bins, coordinating with waste disposal services, and regularly monitoring the effectiveness of the program.
What are the different types of waste? ▶
The different types of waste include organic waste, recyclable waste, hazardous waste, medical waste, and electronic waste (e-waste).
What are ways of storing the waste at homes? ▶
Storing waste at home involves using separate bins for different types of waste, keeping organic waste in sealed containers to prevent odors, and ensuring hazardous waste is stored safely until proper disposal.
How do I dispose my waste? ▶
Disposing of waste properly involves following local waste disposal regulations, recycling recyclable materials, composting organic waste, and using designated drop-off points for hazardous and electronic waste.
